ABOUT NIT KURUKSHETRA

National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra

NIT-KURUKSHETRA

National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra (Formerly Regional Engineering College, established in 1963) is an autonomous technical institution under Ministry of Education, Government of India. In December 2008, it was accredited with the status of Institute of National Importance (INI) by an act of parliament. NIT Kurukshetra is one among the foremost institutes of national importance imparting higher technical education in India. The Institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Engineering and Doctor of Philosophy programme in Engineering, Sciences and Humanities. The Institute enjoys excellent placement opportunities for its undergraduate and postgraduate students.


History

Jawaharlal Nehru, the first Prime Minister of independent India, envisioned India as a leader in science and technology. To realize this vision, the Government of India jointly with the respective state governments founded 14 educational institutions in different regions of India. Such an institution was established in 1963 in Kurukshetra as a joint enterprise of the Government of India and the Government of Punjab as the Regional Engineering College, Kurukshetra (REC Kurukshetra). In 2002, REC Kurukshetra was renamed to National Institute of Technology under the NIT Act and was given a Deemed University status.


Academics

NIT Kurukshetra offers four year undergraduate programme in various areas of engineering and technology. The Institute also offers 2/3 year postgraduate programmes in various areas of engineering, management, and science. Moreover, Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) programmes are offered in engineering, sciences, humanities and social sciences as well as computer applications.


Ranking

National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra was placed at 40th position in the engineering category of NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) ranking, 2020.


Annual Student Activities

These are the major festivals that take place at NIT Kurukshetra every year.

  • Techspardha: Techspardha is a national level annual techno-managerial students’ festival of National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra. It started in 1995 as "Technospect" (later changed to Literati). The year 2013 marked the Golden Jubilee of NIT Kurukshetra, same year it was renamed as Techspardha. Techspardha hosts about 75 competitive events showcasing innovations, startups, creativity of students.

  • Confluence: It is the annual cultural event and one of the most anticipated events in North India. Many colleges from the state as well as from the other parts of the nation take part in Confluence. It is a three-day event and has been visited by some of the most famous artists and personalities of the county in the past few years. For Confluence’19 the theme is ‘Carnival of Heritage’.
